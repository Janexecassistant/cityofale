What the trend says

Split the G is usually judged on the first uninterrupted sip: the foam line should land through the middle bar of the printed G. Looser pub rules sometimes accept the broader printed logo area, but the strict version is more fun to score.

Game recommendations

Use rounds, a visible timer, a precision score, and a near-miss replay. Add a sober practice mode, Guinness 0.0 or any dark stout option, and a house-rule toggle for strict or casual scoring.
